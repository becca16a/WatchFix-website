Fequently Asked Questions

Do you charge for estimates or quotations?

No, our quotation service is totally FREE and you are under no obligation to proceed with the work. If you choose not to proceed after receiving the quotation, there is a small admin fee of Â£25 to pay, and the watch will then be returned back to you in its original condition.

Are you able to repair vintage pocket watches?

Yes, this is an area we are specialists in. Our Master watchmaker is one of the most qualified in the country, and has vast experience is servicing, refurbishing vintage or antique watches and pocket watches.

My watch face / glass has broken, but it is an unusual shape. Can you repair this?

Yes, we are able to cut a glass if required to fit almost any watch. We will need to advise on a cost once we have seen the timepiece.

How do I pay for my repair if I decide to proceed with the estimated work required?

Once you have agreed, and not before, to the work required to repair your timepiece work will commence. We may ask for a deposit from you, and will ask for the balance payment once the work is complete. One of the team will call you at a convenient time to ask for payment, and then your time piece will be posted back to you- of course fully insured.

I have a replica copy watch. Can you repair this?

No, we are unable to work on replica copy watches.

Do you offer a guarantee with repairs?

Yes, 12 months on all repairs (unless otherwise stated), very occasionally things will need adjusting after a repair. We would like to reassure you that, as a four generation family business, we are here to look after you at all times.
